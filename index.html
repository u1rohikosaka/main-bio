Yuichiro "Hiko" Hikosaka is a builder and investor specializing in deep-tech and dual-use technology.

He began his career at Goldman Sachs, where he spent nine years as a trader on the fixed-income and derivatives desks. Transitioning into the startup ecosystem, he served as the founding COO for ventures in robotics and virtual reality.

In venture capital, Hiko was an investor at Airbus Ventures, managing a portfolio across Asia and the U.S. that spanned quantum computing, AI, space, and robotics. He subsequently became a Partner at Soma Capital, a firm that has backed over 40 unicorns and decacorns in the United States.

Currently, Hiko serves as the Head of Global Strategy and Investment at Transcelestial, a world leader in laser communication technology. He holds a B.S. from Keio University and a Master of Economics from The University of Tokyo.

Outside of work, Hiko enjoys playing jazz piano and photography. He is a proud father to two daughters and an Australian Labradoodle named Photon.
